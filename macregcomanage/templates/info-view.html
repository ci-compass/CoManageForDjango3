NOIRLab Wireless Network Registration

Welcome to NOIRLab!

In order to use the wireless network, the "MAC Address" of your computer has to be registered with the network. The name is historical - there is no relationship with any Apple product.

Speaking of which, if you have a Macintosh, bring up the "System Report". Go to the Apple menu in the top left corner, select "About this Mac", and then click on "System Report" in the dialog that pops up. The System Report tool will start up. Look to the list of topics at the left edge of that window. Under "Network", click on "Wi-Fi". It may take several seconds for the tool to respond, but when it does look for the entry corresponding to "MAC Address". The value will consist of pairs of letters and numbers separated by colons (":"). Write this value down. Then enter that value below and click on "Submit".

Note that it may take an hour for your MAC Address to be pushed all the way out to the wireless network, and under some unusual circumstances it could take even longer.
